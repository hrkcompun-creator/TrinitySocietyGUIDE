The Trinity LoreKisah Trinity

A World Shaped by AmbitionDunia yang Dibentuk Ambisi

In the chaotic remnants of Terraria, the Trinity Society was established not as a refuge, but as a crucible. Here, survival is not guaranteed; it is earned. The world is governed by strict cosmic laws—bosses only materialize during specific astrological alignments (Server Time), forcing adventurers to plan, cooperate, and strategize their raids.

Currency here is known as Nityc, a manifestation of defeated evil. By claiming land, establishing trade routes, and evolving your Soul Rank through intense combat, you transform from a mere Outcast into a Trinity Exemplar. But beware: greed has a price, and higher ranks demand heavy taxes in the form of Boss Tokens.

Di tengah sisa-sisa kekacauan Terraria, Trinity Society didirikan bukan sebagai tempat berlindung, melainkan sebagai tempat ujian. Di sini, keselamatan tidak dijamin; ia harus diraih. Dunia ini diatur oleh hukum kosmik ketat—boss hanya muncul saat penyelarasan astrologi tertentu (Waktu Server), memaksa petualang merencanakan dan bekerja sama dalam raid.

Mata uang di sini dikenal sebagai Nityc, wujud dari kejahatan yang ditaklukkan. Dengan mengklaim lahan, membangun rute perdagangan, dan mengevolusikan Rank Jiwa melalui pertempuran sengit, kamu berubah dari seorang Buangan (Outcast) menjadi Trinity Exemplar. Tapi awas: keserakahan ada harganya, dan rank tinggi menuntut pajak berat berupa Token Boss.

Instanced Starter Loot

Loot Peti Instanced

No more empty chests. Every chest generated in the wild provides private, instanced loot for each player. It serves as your personal starter kit.

Tidak ada lagi peti kosong. Setiap peti di alam liar menyediakan loot instanced pribadi untuk setiap pemain sebagai starter kit.

Nityc Economy & Taxes

Ekonomi Nityc & Pajak

Slay bosses to earn Nityc. Use it to buy land (1 Nityc/10 blocks), trade on the Player Market (25% tax), or purchase from the Server Store.

Bunuh boss untuk mendapat Nityc. Gunakan untuk beli lahan (1 Nityc/10 blok), jualan di Pasar Pemain (pajak 25%), atau belanja di Toko Server.

Time-Gated Boss Raids

Jadwal Boss Ketat

Bosses are bound to real-world Server Time schedules. Summoning them outside their window results in immediate despawn. Use /bosslist.

Boss terikat pada jadwal Waktu Server dunia nyata. Memanggil mereka di luar jadwal akan membuatnya langsung lenyap. Pakai /bosslist.

Anti-Spam Quota

Kuota Anti-Spam

You have 5 Global Summons. If quota hits 0, bosses yield 0 EXP & 0 Tokens. Tip: Join someone else's summon (Raid) to save quota!

Kamu punya 5x panggil. Jika kuota 0, boss memberikan 0 EXP & 0 Token. Tips: Mabar/ikut boss panggilan orang lain untuk hemat kuota!

Auto-Clear Sweeper

Pembersih Otomatis

To prevent lag, dropped items are wiped completely every 5 minutes. Warnings appear at 60s and 10s. Keep your inventory organized.

Mencegah lag, item di tanah dihapus permanen setiap 5 menit. Peringatan muncul di 60d dan 10d. Simpan barangmu dengan baik.

VeinMiner Integration

Integrasi VeinMiner

Toggle with /vm. Break one ore block, and the entire connected vein shatters automatically, saving you hours of grinding.

Ketik /vm. Hancurkan satu blok bijih, dan seluruh jalurnya akan hancur otomatis, menghemat waktu menambangmu.

Knowledge BasePusat Bantuan

What happens when my boss quota hits 0/5? Apa yang terjadi jika kuota boss saya 0/5?

You can still summon bosses, but they will yield ZERO EXP and ZERO Tokens. You will only receive Nityc (Money). A 3-hour cooldown timer will automatically start to refill your quota back to 5.

Kamu tetap bisa memanggil boss, namun mereka akan memberikan NOL EXP dan NOL Token. Kamu hanya akan mendapat Nityc (Uang). Timer cooldown 3 jam akan berjalan otomatis untuk mengisi penuh kuotamu.

How is Boss Nityc Money distributed? Bagaimana uang Nityc Boss dibagikan?

To receive money, you must deal at least 2% of the total damage. 50% of the reward pool is given as a base payout, and the remaining 50% scales directly with the percentage of damage you dealt.

Untuk mendapat uang, kamu harus memberikan minimal 2% total damage. 50% dari hadiah dibagikan sebagai bayaran dasar, dan 50% sisanya dibagikan secara proporsional sesuai rasio damage yang kamu berikan.

Why did the boss I summoned disappear instantly? Kenapa boss yang saya panggil langsung hilang?

Bosses are governed by the real-life Server Time schedule. If you summon a boss outside of its designated active window, the Server will forcefully despawn it. Check /bosslist.

Boss diatur oleh jadwal Waktu Server dunia nyata. Jika kamu memanggil boss di luar jam aktifnya, Server akan memaksanya lenyap (despawn). Cek jadwal di /bosslist.
