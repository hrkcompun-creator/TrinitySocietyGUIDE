Server MechanicsMekanisme Server

Understand the laws of the landPahami hukum di tanah ini

Instanced Starter Loot

Loot Peti Pribadi

Every chest generated in the wild provides private, instanced loot for each player. It serves as your personal starter kit.

Setiap peti di alam liar menyediakan loot instanced pribadi untuk setiap pemain sebagai starter kit.

Nityc Economy

Ekonomi Nityc

Slay bosses to earn Nityc. Use it to buy land (1 Nityc/10 blocks), trade on the Player Market (25% tax), or purchase from the Store.

Bunuh boss untuk mendapat Nityc. Gunakan untuk beli lahan (1 Nityc/10 blok) atau jualan di Pasar Pemain (pajak 25%).

Scheduled Boss Raids

Jadwal Raid Ketat

Bosses are bound to real-world Server Time schedules. Summoning them outside their window results in immediate despawn. Use /bosslist.

Boss terikat pada jadwal Waktu Server dunia nyata. Memanggil mereka di luar jadwal akan membuatnya langsung lenyap. Pakai /bosslist.

Anti-Spam Quota

Kuota Anti-Spam

You have 5 Summons. If quota hits 0, bosses yield 0 EXP & 0 Tokens. Tip: Join a friend's summon (Raid) to save quota!

Kamu punya 5x panggil. Jika kuota 0, boss memberikan 0 EXP & 0 Token. Tips: Mabar boss panggilan orang lain untuk hemat kuota!

Auto-Clear Sweeper

Pembersih Otomatis

To prevent lag, dropped items are wiped completely every 5 minutes. Warnings appear at 60s and 10s. Keep your inventory organized.

Mencegah lag, item di tanah dihapus permanen setiap 5 menit. Peringatan muncul di 60d dan 10d. Simpan barangmu dengan baik.

VeinMiner Integration

Integrasi VeinMiner

Toggle with /vm. Break one ore block, and the entire connected vein shatters automatically, saving you hours of grinding.

Ketik /vm. Hancurkan satu blok bijih, dan seluruh jalurnya akan hancur otomatis, menghemat waktu menambangmu.

In-Game Rules

Aturan In-Game

01

No Exploits/Cheats

Dilarang Curang (Cheat)

Mods or dupe glitches result in a permanent ban.

Mod atau eksploitasi duplikat = Ban Permanen.

02

Respect Claimed Land

Hargai Lahan Pemain

Do not trap or lava-cast other players' safe zones.

Dilarang menjebak/menyiram lava ke rumah orang.

03

Adhere to Quotas

Patuhi Kuota Boss

Do not bypass the 5-summon limit.

Dilarang menembus batas 5x panggil boss.

04

Secure Your Items

Amankan Barangmu

Items lost to the 5-minute Auto-Clear are not refunded.

Item yang lenyap oleh Auto-Clear tidak dikembalikan.

05

No AFK Exploitation

Dilarang AFK Farm

Automated AFK mob farms causing lag will be destroyed.

Mesin AFK farm pembuat lag akan dihancurkan.

06

PvP Consent Required

PvP Harus Sepakat

Random killing without consent is prohibited.

Membunuh pemain secara acak tanpa izin dilarang.

07

Map Preservation

Jaga Kelestarian Map

Do not randomly destroy natural landscapes or oceans.

Jangan menghancurkan pemandangan tanpa alasan.

08

Report Bugs Promptly

Laporkan Bug Segera

Exploiting a plugin bug leads to punishment.

Memanfaatkan bug sistem akan berujung hukuman.

Community Rules

Aturan Komunitas

09

Maintain Respect

Jaga Sopan Santun

Zero tolerance for racism, hate speech, or harassment.

Toleransi nol untuk rasisme dan ujaran kebencian.

10

No Spam / Advertising

Dilarang Spam/Iklan

Do not promote other servers in the groups.

Dilarang promosi server lain di grup WA/Discord.

11

Official Trading Only

Hanya Transaksi Resmi

Use the in-game /market system to avoid getting scammed.

Gunakan sistem /market in-game agar aman.

12

Respect Staff

Hargai Staf Admin

Admins have the final say. Do not argue in global chat.

Keputusan Admin mutlak. Jangan berdebat di chat.

13

No Impersonation

Dilarang Menyamar

Pretending to be Staff or another player is forbidden.

Berpura-pura menjadi Admin/pemain lain dilarang.

14

Strictly SFW Content

Konten Wajib SFW

No NSFW, gore, or highly inappropriate content.

Dilarang mengirim konten NSFW/dewasa di grup.

15

Language Constraints

Aturan Bahasa

Keep global chat readable. Use English or Indonesian.

Gunakan bahasa Inggris atau Indonesia di chat global.

16

Have Fun & Assist

Bermain & Membantu

Help newcomers understand the hardcore mechanics.

Bantu pemain baru memahami mekanisme hardcore.
