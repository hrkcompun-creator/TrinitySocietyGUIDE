Server MechanicsMekanisme Server

Understand the laws of the landPahami hukum di tanah ini

Instanced Starter Loot

Loot Peti Pribadi

Chests generated in the wild provide private, instanced loot for each player as a starter kit.

Peti di alam liar menyediakan loot instanced pribadi untuk setiap pemain sebagai starter kit.

Nityc Economy

Ekonomi Nityc

Slay bosses to earn Nityc. Buy land (1 Nityc/10 blocks) or trade on the Player Market (25% tax).

Bunuh boss untuk Nityc. Beli lahan (1 Nityc/10 blok) atau jualan di Pasar Pemain (pajak 25%).

Scheduled Boss Raids

Jadwal Raid Ketat

Bosses are bound to Server Time schedules. Summoning them outside their window results in despawn.

Boss terikat pada jadwal Waktu Server. Memanggil mereka di luar jadwal akan lenyap.

Anti-Spam Quota

Kuota Anti-Spam

You have 5 Summons. At 0 quota, bosses yield 0 EXP/Tokens. Tip: Co-op Raid to save quota!

Maksimal 5x panggil. Kuota 0 = Boss tak beri EXP/Token. Tips: Ikut raid teman!

Auto-Clear Sweeper

Pembersih Otomatis

Dropped items are wiped every 5 minutes (60s/10s warnings). Keep inventory organized.

Item di tanah dihapus tiap 5 menit (ada peringatan 60d/10d). Amankan barangmu.

VeinMiner Integration

Integrasi VeinMiner

Toggle with /vm. Break one ore block, and the entire vein shatters automatically.

Ketik /vm. Hancurkan satu blok bijih, dan seluruh jalurnya hancur otomatis.

In-Game Rules

Aturan In-Game

01

No Exploits/Cheats

Dilarang Curang (Cheat)

Mods or dupe glitches result in permanent ban.

Mod atau eksploitasi duplikat = Ban Permanen.

02

Respect Claimed Land

Hargai Lahan Pemain

Do not trap or lava-cast players' safe zones.

Dilarang menjebak/menyiram lava ke rumah orang.

03

Adhere to Quotas

Patuhi Kuota Boss

Do not bypass the 5-summon limit.

Dilarang menembus batas 5x panggil boss.

04

Secure Your Items

Amankan Barangmu

Items lost to Auto-Clear are not refunded.

Item lenyap oleh Auto-Clear tak dikembalikan.

05

No AFK Exploitation

Dilarang AFK Farm

Automated AFK mob farms will be destroyed.

Mesin AFK farm pembuat lag akan dihancurkan.

06

PvP Consent Required

PvP Harus Sepakat

Random killing without consent is prohibited.

Membunuh acak tanpa izin dilarang keras.

07

Map Preservation

Jaga Kelestarian Map

Do not randomly destroy natural landscapes.

Jangan hancurkan pemandangan tanpa alasan.

08

Report Bugs

Laporkan Bug

Exploiting a plugin bug leads to punishment.

Memanfaatkan bug sistem akan berujung hukuman.
